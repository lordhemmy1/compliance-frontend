FAQ

QUESTIONS WE
HEAR MOST OFTEN

Do I need to know my expiry dates to sign up?+
Not at all. After signup we send you a simple compliance audit form. Fill in what you know — we handle the rest. For industry-standard items we can pre-populate common renewal cycles and follow up on any missing dates.
Which countries do you support?+
We currently support businesses in the UK, United States, Canada, Australia, Ireland, New Zealand, and Nigeria. We have industry-specific compliance checklists pre-built for each country.
How do I receive my alerts?+
Depending on your plan, alerts are delivered via email, WhatsApp message, SMS, and Google Calendar notifications. Our Standard and Premium plans include WhatsApp — the highest open-rate channel available.
Can I add my own compliance items?+
Yes. We pre-load standard items for your industry, but you can add any custom compliance item — from insurance policies to internal training deadlines — with your own renewal cycle.
Is there a contract or minimum term?+
No contract, no minimum term. All plans are month-to-month and can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. We keep you because the service works, not because you're locked in.
How is my data kept secure?+
Your compliance data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties. Each client's data is isolated and only accessible by you and our support team when you request assistance.
